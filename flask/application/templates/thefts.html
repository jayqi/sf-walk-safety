Street Thefts

Thefts are the crime of stealing possessions, including pickpocketing and pursesnatching. Using open data from the San Francisco Police department, we visualize street theft incidents since 2003 and examine their prevalence.

In the adjacent figure, we see the total number of reported street theft incidents from 2003 to 2015.
