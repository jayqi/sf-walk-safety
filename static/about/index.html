SF Walk Safety

A data-driven examination of pedestrian safety in San Francisco.

About the Project

Being new to San Francisco and planning to walk around a lot by himself, Jay wanted to learn more about walking in the city. With available open government data, this was a good opportunity to apply data analysis and machine learning.

This project was developed as part of the Data Incubator, an eight-week data science fellowship program for training advanced-degree graduates to enter industry.
